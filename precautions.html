Precautions

"Prevention is better than Cure". It's always easier to stop something happening in the first place than to repair the damage after it has happened. In the same way, let us see what we can follow to prevent Covid-19, instead of worrying for the cure.

  • Wear a mask.
  • Stay at least 1 metre away from others, even if they donâ€™t appear to be sick, since people can have the virus without having symptoms.
  • Avoid crowded places, poorly ventilated, indoor locations and avoid prolonged contact with others.
  • Avoid touching surfaces, in case people infected with COVID-19 have touched them.
  • Cover your coughs and sneezes with a bent elbow or tissue.
  • Frequently clean your hands with soap and water, or a sanitizer.
  • Get vaccinated.
